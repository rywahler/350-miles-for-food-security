About the ride

Biking the GAP and C&O trails has been a dream of mine since I started school in Pittsburgh. I figured I should put some meaning into the ride, I am calling on family, friends, and all others to donate just a couple cents per mile to make it a worthwhile experience. The goal is to raise $10 per mile, which over approximately 350 miles comes to $3,500.

Volunteering with the Maryland Food Bank has been some of my most rewarding experiences, including gleaning produce from local farms to packing and distribution at the food bank's warehouse. Riding through the neighborhoods of Pittsburgh I have seen a need to support the food banks here as well. In Maryland an increased cost of living and food in particular, leaves 40% of food insecure Marylanders working full time jobs and earning too much to qualify for federal food assistance. Pittsburgh has seen immense revitalization with the growth of its tech hub. But Pittsburgh is a city of neighborhoods, and while some have thrived in the revitalization, other neighborhoods have lagged with the loss of heavy industry.

Donating to the Maryland Food Bank or the Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank will give you a chance to appear on the leaderboard I composed. Both the Maryland Food Bank and the Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank are extremely effective in their work. The Maryland Food Bank has a 90% resource efficiency rate, meaning that 90% of their funds go directly to food distribution - rather than admin or fundraising costs. The Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank ensures that for every dollar donated, 3 meals are provided to those in need.
